"For here or to go?" she felt herself say automatically.

             Another scoop of potatoes, another bunch of nuggets, and finally the ever evasive vegetables. Eventually each person was simply another scoop, another bunch, and another rejection of vegetables. Sometimes she heard them whisper about her, saying, "Did you see these portions? How can they expect me to get full on this? She was so slow." She tried to tune those people out. It was her first day for goodness’ sake. How was she supposed to know how much food to give?
