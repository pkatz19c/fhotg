"Excuse me I said no vegetables." She was pulled out of her reverie from an annoyed male tone. "Can I get extra potatoes too? I'm really hungry." You would think these people hadn't eaten for weeks from the amount of potatoes they asked for. She put another half a scoop on the plate and looked up to find him glaring at her.

"Have a nice day" she quietly told him as he walked away. Then, "For here or to go?"
